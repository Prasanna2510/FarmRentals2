GET STARTED

We open the door to thousands of farmers. Rent your equipment for the off-season, and create an advertisement on out plaform. Through out rigorous customer compliance, we make sure that only reliable users gain access to our digital marketplace. There are two ways to get started:

Post ads as Owner*


As an owner, start posting rent offers for the agricultural equipments by providing available timeline to the buyers/customers and get rapid reactions from nearby interested customers.


*Register now and start posting Ads with one click.

Post as a Customer*


As a customer, start exploring and filter the various agricultural equipments for you. Communicate with the owner DIRECTLY for offers, and book great deals while experiencing satisfaction.


*Start exploring the equipments with one click.
